Analytics Unit Programmes

The Unit consists of analysts, economists, data scientists, data engineers, data developers and a comms manager that sit across two workstreams.

Innovation Analytics

Collect, generate and advise on best practice for conducting and procuring innovative analytics within the health and care sector in a valid, transparent and interoperable way.

Programme Analytics

Ensure the right information, is available to the right decision makers, at the right time, and in the right context.
